Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: July 2026

At SpeakEasy, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so using one of the methods below.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your learning progress, achievements, and personal data will be permanently removed.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly within the app:

  1. Open the SpeakEasy app on your device
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice when prompted

Your account and data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to make a manual request, send an email to our support team:

  • Email: keshabsarkar2018@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - SpeakEasy

Please include the following information in your email:

  • The email address associated with your SpeakEasy account
  • Your registered display name (if available)
  • A brief request stating you want your account deleted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Learning progress (grammar lessons, quiz scores, vocabulary history)
  • Game scores, streaks, achievements, and XP
  • Mock test results and performance analytics
  • App usage statistics and preferences
✅ Note: Speaking practice recordings are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers, so no additional deletion is needed for those.

What Is Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes:

  • Anonymized usage analytics (with all personal identifiers removed)
  • Transaction records if applicable (for tax/legal compliance)

Processing Time

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate
  • Email Request: Within 7 business days of receiving your request
  • Backup Data: Permanently deleted within 30 days

Data Safety on Google Play

As required by Google Play's Data Safety section, we confirm that:

  • Account deletion is available both in-app and via request
  • All user data associated with the account is deleted upon request
  • No residual personal data is retained beyond the 30-day backup retention period
