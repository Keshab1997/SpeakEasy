Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Thank you for choosing SpeakEasy ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name via Firebase Authentication. You may also sign in using Google Sign-In.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional), learning preferences, and language level.

1.2 Learning Data

  • Grammar lesson progress, quiz scores, vocabulary learning history
  • Speaking practice recordings (processed locally on your device)
  • Game scores, streaks, achievements, and XP
  • Mock test results and performance analytics

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App version and usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our language learning services
  • To track your learning progress and personalize your experience
  • To send push notifications for learning reminders (with your consent)
  • To improve our app through analytics
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Cloud Firestore (database), Cloud Storage, Analytics, Remote Config, and Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertising
  • OneSignal: Push notifications
  • Google Sign-In: Social authentication (optional)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing data handling practices.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your learning progress is stored locally on your device using Hive and synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Speech recordings are processed in real-time on your device and are not stored on our servers

5. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data about your device and app usage to deliver targeted ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can access your account data within the app
  • Correction: You can update your profile information
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data through the app settings
  • Opt-Out: You can disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Notifications: You can manage notification preferences in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

SpeakEasy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
