📱 How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

You can delete your account directly from within the SpeakEasy app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the SpeakEasy app on your device
  2. Go to Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap on Settings (⚙️ icon)
  4. Go to Privacy & Security
  5. Tap on Delete Account
  6. Confirm your choice when prompted
  7. Enter your password for verification (if using email login)
