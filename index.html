Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

SpeakEasy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our app, we may collect:

  • Name — to personalize your learning experience
  • Email Address — for account authentication and communication
  • Profile Photo — if you choose to upload one

1.2 Learning Data

We collect data related to your learning activities:

  • Lessons completed, quiz scores, and learning progress
  • Practice history and speaking exercises
  • Game scores, XP points, coins, and achievements
  • Daily streaks and activity patterns

1.3 Device Information

We may collect certain device information automatically:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage statistics and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our English learning services
  • Personalize your learning experience and track your progress
  • Send you practice reminders and learning notifications (with your consent)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and content
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using Hive database technology for offline access.

3.2 Cloud Storage

When you sign in, your data is synced to Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) for backup and cross-device access. Data is stored on secure servers in compliance with industry standards.

3.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, Cloud Storage, and Analytics
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method
  • AI API Providers — If you configure an API key, requests are sent to your chosen provider (e.g., OpenAI, OpenRouter). We do not store your API keys on our servers.

5. Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone — for speaking practice and pronunciation exercises
  • Camera/Photos — for updating your profile picture (optional)
  • Notifications — for daily practice reminders and learning alerts

These permissions are used only for the stated purposes and can be revoked at any time from your device settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Export your learning data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Clear locally stored data from the app settings

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us using the information below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and learning data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
